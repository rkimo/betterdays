Better Days Clinic ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you provide through our website. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit betterdaysclinic.ca.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only as necessary to operate and improve our website. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you fill out the contact form, we collect the personal information you provide, such as your name, email address, and any other details you include.
  • Cookies and Analytics Data: We use cookies for analytics and statistical purposes to improve the website’s functionality and understand how visitors engage with our website. These cookies collect non-personal information, such as IP address, device type, and browsing activity on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to Inquiries: Personal information from the contact form is used to respond to your questions and provide information as requested.
  • Website Analytics: Non-personal information collected via cookies helps us analyze website traffic, optimize the user experience, and improve our website’s content.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to help us analyze website traffic and maintain our site. These third parties are bound by privacy agreements that prevent them from using your information for any purpose other than supporting our website.

4. Your Choices

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain website features. If you submit a contact form and later wish to withdraw your information, please reach out to us at info@betterdaysclinic.ca.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the personal information you provide from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting those sites.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page, with the effective date noted at the top.
