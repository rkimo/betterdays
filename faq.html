Clinic FAQ


No referral required. Most self-refer for care. If you have a family physician, we are pleased to accept their referral.

All medical services are covered by your valid OHIP card.

Each visit all patients will be treated by an experienced Doctor certified in addiction medicine.

Regular visits are face to face/in person. Telehealth/virtual care is offered periodically according to patient need.

We can assist if you have legal problems related to substances/drugs/alcohol. Justice, penal, and legal systems look more favourably on those with a problem who are in treatment.

MTO/Ministry of Transportation Ontario Substance Use reviews for driving privileges may be completed when indicated (additional fees may apply).

ODSP (Ontario Disability Support Programs) applications will be completed when indicated for regular clinic patients.

We will support your family by working with FACS Niagara (Family And Children Services) if they are involved with your family and if a child may be at risk.
